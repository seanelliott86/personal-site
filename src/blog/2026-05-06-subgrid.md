Subgrid is a game changer and makes your life easier.\
\
If you look at my site now youtr going to see a flatter structure and thats thanks to subgrid.

Previously i defined a reusable grid with different widths for different bits of content. The problem was, for each section I had to recreate that grid and then append another class to say what columns it worked too.

Sure i made it rusable and uses the same class but now. I can define my grid at the top level and subgrid it to align to the columns i want
